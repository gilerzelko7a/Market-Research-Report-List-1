<p><p>The ablation equipment market encompasses various technologies used for minimally invasive procedures targeting tissue destruction. Ultrasound ablation utilizes high-frequency sound waves to generate heat, effectively destroying unwanted tissues, often used in cosmetic and therapeutic applications. Radiofrequency ablation, on the other hand, employs electrical energy to produce heat, targeting conditions like cardiac arrhythmias and tumors. Both methods offer significant benefits, such as reduced recovery times and lower risks compared to traditional surgical approaches, driving their demand in clinical settings.</p></p>
