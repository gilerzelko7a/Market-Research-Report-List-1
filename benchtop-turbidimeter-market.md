<p><p>The benchtop turbidimeter market is segmented into two primary types: those without RFID and those with RFID technology. Instruments without RFID rely on traditional measurement methods, typically using standard calibration procedures for turbidity analysis. In contrast, models with RFID integrate radio-frequency identification for enhanced automation, allowing for easier data tracking, storage, and retrieval. This integration improves efficiency and accuracy, making RFID-equipped turbidimeters suitable for environments where rapid and precise monitoring is essential, such as research laboratories and quality control settings.</p></p>
