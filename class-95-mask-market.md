<p><p>Class 95 masks include three types based on filtration efficiency: N95, P95, and R95. N95 masks filter out at least 95% of airborne particles but are not resistant to oil. P95 masks also filter 95% of particles and are oil-proof, suitable for workplace environments with oil-based aerosols. R95 masks filter 95% of particles and are resistant to oil, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Each type is designed for specific environments and exposure levels, ensuring adequate respiratory protection.</p></p>
