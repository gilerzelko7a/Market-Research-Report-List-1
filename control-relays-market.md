<p><p>The control relays market includes various types that serve distinct purposes. Impulse relays generate brief electrical pulses for switching applications, enabling precise control in automation systems. Monitoring relays continuously observe electrical parameters, providing critical data for system diagnostics and safety. Signal conditioning relays process and modify signals to ensure compatibility between different components in a control system, enhancing performance and reliability. Together, these relays play a vital role in improving automation efficiency and operational safety.</p></p>
