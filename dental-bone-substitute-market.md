<p><strong>The Dental Bone Substitute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bone substitute market is primarily categorized into three types: allograft, xenograft, and synthetic grafts. Allografts utilize human bone from a donor, offering good biological integration and reducing the risk of disease transmission. Xenografts are derived from animal bone, often cow or pig, providing a scaffold for bone regeneration and minimizing immune response. Both types are crucial in dental procedures, particularly in implantology, where bone augmentation is needed for successful outcomes. Synthetic grafts are also gaining traction, offering customizable options.</p></p>
