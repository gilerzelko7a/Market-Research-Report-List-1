<p><strong>The Dental Impression Trays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>For Partial Dentures</li><li>For Complete Dentures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental impression trays market is categorized into two main types: partial denture trays and complete denture trays. Partial denture trays are designed to capture impressions for patients needing replacement teeth for some missing teeth, ensuring precise fit and comfort. In contrast, complete denture trays are used for edentulous patients, capturing the entire dental arch to create full dentures. Both types are essential for effective dental restoration, influencing the quality of prosthetics and patient satisfaction in dental care.</p></p>
