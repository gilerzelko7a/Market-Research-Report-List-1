<p><p>Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) machines are categorized into several types based on imaging techniques. X-ray Angiography uses X-rays to visualize blood vessels and guides interventional procedures. CT Angiography employs computed tomography for detailed, cross-sectional images of blood vessels. Magnetic Resonance Angiography utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images without radiation exposure. MR-CT Angiography combines both techniques, leveraging the strengths of magnetic resonance and computed tomography for enhanced vascular imaging and diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
