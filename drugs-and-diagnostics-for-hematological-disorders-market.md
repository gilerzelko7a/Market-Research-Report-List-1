<p><p>The Drugs and Diagnostics for Hematological Disorders market encompasses two primary types: analyzers and reagents. Reagents, on the other hand, are chemical substances used in laboratory testing to react with blood samples, facilitating the detection and analysis of specific components, thereby enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
