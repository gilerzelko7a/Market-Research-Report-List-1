<p><p>The DTC genetic testing market is categorized into three primary types. Targeted analysis focuses on specific genes or mutations associated with hereditary conditions or traits. Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) chips assess genetic variations across many points in the genome, providing insights into ancestry and disease susceptibility. Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) offers a comprehensive view by analyzing an individual's entire genetic makeup, revealing extensive genomic information for various applications, including health risks and personalized medicine. Each type serves distinct consumer needs.</p></p>
