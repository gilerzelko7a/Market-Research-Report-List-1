<p><p>Electronic Records Management (ERM) systems are designed to organize, store, and manage digital records efficiently. The market for ERM can be divided into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise ERM systems are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data but requiring significant maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based ERM solutions leverage internet connectivity to provide scalable storage and accessibility, minimizing infrastructure costs and allowing for easier collaboration and updates, making them increasingly popular among organizations.</p></p>
