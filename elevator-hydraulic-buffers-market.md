<p><p>Elevator hydraulic buffers are essential components that enhance the safety and performance of hydraulic elevators. The market can be categorized into two types: tap and vertical. Tap buffers are designed to absorb shock at various points along the elevator shaft, effectively managing energy dissipation during emergency landings. Vertical buffers, on the other hand, are specifically positioned at the base of the shaft to cushion the elevator car during descent, ensuring passenger safety and mechanical integrity in critical situations.</p></p>
