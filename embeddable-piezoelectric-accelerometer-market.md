<p><ul><li>PE Type</li><li>IEPE Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embeddable Piezoelectric Accelerometer market can be categorized into two main types: PE (Piezoceramic Element) Type and IEPE (Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric) Type. The PE Type utilizes a piezoelectric crystal for high-frequency detection, ideal for applications requiring precision in dynamic measurements. In contrast, IEPE Type integrates electronic circuitry within the accelerometer, simplifying signal processing and enhancing sensitivity, particularly for low-frequency signals. Both types are crucial for applications in aerospace, automotive, and structural health monitoring, where vibration analysis is essential.</p></p>
