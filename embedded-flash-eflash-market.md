<p><strong>The Embedded Flash (eFlash) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Level Cell (SLC)</li><li>Multi-Level Cell (MLC)</li><li>Triple Level Cell (TLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded Flash (eFlash) market types include Single Level Cell (SLC), Multi-Level Cell (MLC), and Triple Level Cell (TLC). SLC stores one bit per cell, offering high performance, durability, and lower power consumption, making it ideal for industrial and automotive applications. MLC stores two bits per cell, balancing cost and performance, suitable for consumer electronics. TLC, which stores three bits per cell, provides higher storage density and lower cost per gigabyte, making it popular for mass storage applications, though with reduced endurance and speed.</p></p>
