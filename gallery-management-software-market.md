<p><p>Gallery management software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the system via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing full control over data and security but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve to optimize operations in galleries, enhancing collection management and visitor engagement.</p></p>
