<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power CT Tube market is primarily categorized into two types: stationary anode tubes and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed anode that provides consistent imaging but may have limitations in heat dissipation and imaging speed. In contrast, rotating anode tubes enhance performance by utilizing a rotating target, allowing for better heat management, faster imaging, and higher-quality scans. This differentiation caters to varying diagnostic needs in medical imaging and industrial applications.</p></p>
