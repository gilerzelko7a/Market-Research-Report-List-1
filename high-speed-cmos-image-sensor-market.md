<p><strong>The High Speed CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Speed CMOS Image Sensor market is primarily divided into Front-Illuminated (FI) and Backside-Irradiated (BSI) CMOS sensors. FI CMOS sensors capture light through the front surface, typically yielding lower sensitivity in low-light conditions. In contrast, BSI CMOS sensors enhance light collection by placing the photodiode on the back side, allowing for improved performance in low-light environments and higher overall sensitivity. This differentiation caters to diverse applications, from consumer electronics to industrial imaging, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
