<p><ul><li>Gear Hydraulic Starter</li><li>Piston Hydraulic Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic starter market consists primarily of two types: gear hydraulic starters and piston hydraulic starters. Gear hydraulic starters utilize a gear mechanism to convert hydraulic energy into rotational motion, offering reliable starting for engines in heavy machinery. Piston hydraulic starters, on the other hand, leverage piston movement to generate high-pressure hydraulic force, ideal for high-torque applications. Both types serve crucial roles in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors, enhancing efficiency and performance in starting large engines.</p></p>
