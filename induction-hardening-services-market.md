<p><p>Induction hardening services encompass three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Surface Hardening** involves heating just the outer layer of a component, enhancing wear resistance while maintaining a softer core for toughness.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Through Hardening** treats the entire component, ensuring uniform hardness throughout, suitable for high-strength applications.</p><p>3. **Selective Hardening** targets specific areas of a part, allowing for tailored properties where needed, balancing strength and ductility effectively. Each method caters to different industrial requirements for durability and performance.</p></p>
