<p><p>Industrial vacuum pumps are essential for various applications, categorized mainly into three types. Dry vacuum pumps operate without lubrication, making them suitable for clean environments and minimizing contamination. Liquid ring vacuum pumps utilize a liquid to create a seal, ideal for handling wet gases and providing efficient operation in challenging conditions. Rotary vane vacuum pumps use sliding vanes to create a vacuum, offering reliable performance for a wide range of industrial processes, from packaging to chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
