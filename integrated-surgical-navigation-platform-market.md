<p><p>The Integrated Surgical Navigation Platform market encompasses two primary types: Hybrid Navigation and Optical Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines various tracking techniques, such as electromagnetic and optical systems, to enhance surgical precision and versatility across diverse applications. In contrast, Optical Navigation utilizes advanced vision systems and markers to provide real-time, 3D visual guidance during procedures, ensuring accurate tool placement. Both technologies aim to improve surgical outcomes by offering surgeons reliable support and enhancing procedural efficiency in various medical disciplines.</p></p>
