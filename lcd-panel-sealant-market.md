<p><p>The LCD panel sealant market is categorized into high viscosity and low viscosity segments based on the thickness of the sealant used. High viscosity sealants are thicker, providing robust adhesion and superior durability, making them ideal for sealing edges and ensuring long-lasting performance in demanding environments. In contrast, low viscosity sealants are thinner, allowing for easier application and better flow, suitable for intricate designs or smaller components. Both types play essential roles in enhancing the performance and longevity of LCD panels.</p></p>
