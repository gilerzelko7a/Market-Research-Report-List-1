<p><ul><li>Single Luciferase Assay Reagent</li><li>Double-luciferase Assay Reagent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luciferase assay reagent market is primarily divided into two types: single luciferase assay reagents and double luciferase assay reagents. Single luciferase assay reagents are designed for detecting the activity of a single luciferase enzyme, typically used for straightforward applications. In contrast, double luciferase assay reagents enable the simultaneous measurement of two luciferase activities, providing a comparative analysis of gene expression or cellular activity. This dual approach enhances sensitivity and allows for more complex experimental designs in research and drug discovery.</p></p>
