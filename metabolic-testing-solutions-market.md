<p><ul><li>VO2 Max Analysis</li><li>RMR Analysis</li><li>Body Composition Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metabolic testing solutions include three primary market types: VO2 Max analysis, RMR analysis, and body composition analysis. VO2 Max analysis measures the maximum oxygen uptake during exercise, indicating cardiovascular fitness and endurance potential. RMR analysis determines the resting metabolic rate, providing insights into calorie expenditure at rest and helping in weight management. Body composition analysis evaluates the proportions of fat, muscle, and water in the body, essential for understanding overall health and tailoring fitness programs effectively. Together, they offer comprehensive metabolic insights for individuals and professionals.</p></p>
