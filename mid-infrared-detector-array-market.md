<p><p>The mid-infrared detector array market includes various types of detectors such as Indium Antimonide (InSb), Lead Selenide (PbSe), and Lead Sulfide (PbS). InSb detectors are known for their high sensitivity and fast response times, making them suitable for applications like spectroscopy and thermal imaging. PbSe detectors have a wide spectral response and are often used in environmental monitoring and gas detection. PbS detectors, while less sensitive, are cost-effective and suitable for applications in the near-infrared range.</p></p>
