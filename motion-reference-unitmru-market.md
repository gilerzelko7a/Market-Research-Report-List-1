<p><p>A Motion Reference Unit (MRU) is a device that measures motion and orientation, often using sensors like accelerometers and gyroscopes. The market for MRUs is categorized into single-axis and dual-axis systems. Single-axis MRUs measure motion along a single direction, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, dual-axis MRUs capture motion across two axes, providing more comprehensive data for complex applications such as robotics and aerospace. Both types enhance navigation and stability in various technological fields.</p></p>
