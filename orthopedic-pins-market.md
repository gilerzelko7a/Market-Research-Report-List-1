<p><strong>The Orthopedic Pins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Absorbable</li><li>Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic pins market is segmented into two main types: non-absorbable and absorbable pins. Non-absorbable pins are designed for long-term support, providing stable fixation that remains in the body until surgically removed, making them suitable for complex fractures. In contrast, absorbable pins are made from materials that gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for a second surgery. This type is often used in pediatric cases or minor surgeries, promoting healing while minimizing complications from foreign materials.</p></p>
