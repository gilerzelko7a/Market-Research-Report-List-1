<p><strong>The Plastic Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Connections</li><li>Flanged Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li><li>Socked Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic ball valves market is categorized based on connection types: </p><p>1. **Threaded Connections** are easy to install and commonly used in low-pressure applications. </p><p>2. **Flanged Connections** provide secure fittings for larger pipes, suitable for high-pressure systems. </p><p>3. **Butt Weld Connections** enable strong joints by welding valve ends to pipes, ideal for permanent installations. </p><p>4. **Socket Weld Connections** allow for seamless integration in piping systems, ensuring minimal pressure loss, typically used in high-pressure applications. Each type serves specific needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
