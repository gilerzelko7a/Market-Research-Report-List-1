<p><p>Platform screen doors (PSDs) are safety features at subway stations that prevent accidents and improve efficiency. The market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Full-Closed Type**: These are solid barriers that completely enclose the platform, enhancing safety and climate control. </p><p>2. **Semi-Closed Type**: These feature partial barriers providing a balance of safety and ventilation, often with sliding doors aligned with train doors. </p><p>3. **Half Height Type**: These are shorter barriers that offer some protection while allowing for easier passenger accessibility and visibility. Each type serves specific requirements based on station designs and safety regulations.</p></p>
