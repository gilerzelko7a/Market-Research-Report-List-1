<p><strong>The Poultry Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural air Flow System</li><li>Mechanical Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The poultry ventilation systems market primarily comprises two types: natural air flow systems and mechanical ventilation systems. Natural air flow systems rely on wind and temperature differences to facilitate air movement, promoting energy efficiency and lower operational costs. In contrast, mechanical ventilation systems utilize fans and exhaust systems to control airflow, ensuring consistent ventilation regardless of external conditions. Both systems aim to maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels in poultry houses, enhancing animal welfare and productivity while minimizing disease risks.</p></p>
