<p><ul><li>Key-type drill chucks</li><li>Keyless drill chucks</li><li>Self-tightening drill chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power tool drill chucks market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Key-type drill chucks** use a key to secure the drill bit, ensuring a tight grip but requiring an additional tool for adjustments. </p><p>2. **Keyless drill chucks** allow for easy bit changes by hand, offering convenience and speed for users. </p><p>3. **Self-tightening drill chucks** automatically tighten the bit during operation, improving grip and reducing slippage, enhancing overall efficiency. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences in drilling applications.</p></p>
