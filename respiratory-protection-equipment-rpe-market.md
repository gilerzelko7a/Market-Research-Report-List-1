<p><strong>The Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure APR</li><li>Positive Pressure PAPR</li><li>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) encompasses various devices designed to safeguard users from airborne contaminants. Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirators (APR) filter out pollutants as users inhale, relying on a tight fit against the face. Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR) use a fan to supply purified air, creating a higher pressure inside the mask, ensuring contaminants cannot enter. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) provide an independent air supply, often used in hazardous environments, allowing users to breathe safely without reliance on external air.</p></p>
