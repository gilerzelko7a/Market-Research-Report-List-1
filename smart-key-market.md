<p><p>The Smart Key Market is divided into two primary types based on sensor technology: Capacitive Sensor Technology and Infra-Red Sensor Technology. Capacitive sensors detect touch or proximity by measuring changes in electric charge, offering high responsiveness and accuracy for keyless entry systems. In contrast, Infra-Red sensors utilize infrared light to detect the presence of an object, enhancing security through remote activation and reduced accidental unlocking. Both technologies contribute to the growing demand for convenient and secure vehicle access solutions.</p></p>
