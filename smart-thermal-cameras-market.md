<p><p>The Smart Thermal Cameras market is segmented into three types based on wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave cameras. Short-wave length cameras operate in the 1-3 micrometer range, ideal for detecting high-temperature objects and are commonly used in industrial inspections. Mid-wave length cameras, ranging from 3-5 micrometers, are effective in various surveillance applications and environmental monitoring. Long-wave length cameras, operating from 8-14 micrometers, excel in low-light conditions, making them suitable for building inspections and military use. Each type caters to specific applications, enhancing their utility in diverse sectors.</p></p>
