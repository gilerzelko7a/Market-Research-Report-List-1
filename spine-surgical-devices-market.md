<p><strong>The Spine Surgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spinal Fusion Products</li><li>Non-fusion Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spine surgical devices market is categorized into two main types: spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are designed to permanently join two or more vertebrae, providing stability and alleviating pain, often through implants like screws and rods. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, aim to stabilize the spine without permanently merging vertebrae, using devices like artificial discs and spinal braces. These segments address different patient needs and conditions, influencing treatment options in spine surgery.</p></p>
