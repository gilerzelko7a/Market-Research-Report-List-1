<p><p>The thin-film solar cell market comprises three main types: CdTe, CIS/CIGS, and a-Si. CIS/CIGS cells use copper indium selenide or copper indium gallium selenide, known for their high efficiency and flexibility. a-Si thin-film cells employ amorphous silicon, providing lightweight, reduced material usage, and good performance in low light. Each type caters to specific applications and market needs, driving innovation in solar technology.</p></p>
