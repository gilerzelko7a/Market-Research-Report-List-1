<p><p>In the power market, valve types are categorized primarily into quarter-turn valves, multi-turn valves, control valves, and others. Quarter-turn valves operate with a 90-degree turn to open or close, making them suitable for quick shut-off. Multi-turn valves require several turns to operate, ideal for fine flow regulation. Control valves are designed for precise flow management, often used in process control applications. The "others" category includes specialized valves, such as safety and relief valves, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
